--- title: "Projects" --- # Projects ## AMAL Initiative: SRH Service Utilization Analysis This project analyzed changes in healthcare utilization among adolescent mothers in Somalia using baseline and endline survey data. I used statistical modeling to assess whether service use improved and explored how factors like education influenced outcomes. **Tools:** R, data cleaning, logistic regression, data visualization [View Project](https://github.com/sarambaji/amal-dashboard) --- ## AI Governance Document Classification This project built a machine learning pipeline to automatically classify AI governance documents into key domains such as bias, privacy, and safety. The goal was to reduce manual review time and create a scalable way to organize large volumes of policy and regulatory documents. **Tools:** Python, Pandas, NLP, Sentence Transformers [View Project](https://github.com/sarambaji/ai-governance-classification)
